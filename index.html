TOGETHER WE CAN

Welcome to Meyerholz Community Association, a non-profit community organization dedicated to improving the lives of those in our community through various initiatives and programs. With the support of volunteers and donors, we strive to make a positive impact and create a better future for all. Join us in our efforts to make a difference and build a stronger community.

    Our History

    Meyerholz Elementary was founded in 1957, has 65 years of rich history and a CA distinguished elementary school, and was permanently closed. The community is very sad about this decision, and we formed this organization to connect the community members and help the families and kids that are in need.

  • {% include icons/site.html icon="conditions" %}

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to empower and improve the lives of individuals and families in our community by providing access to resources, promoting education and growth, and fostering a supportive environment that celebrates diversity and inclusivity.

Sunday Volunteer Service
